About Me

Background & Mission

I am a highly motivated researcher in Computer Science with a solid foundation in academia and a passion for bridging Artificial Intelligence with industrial systems. Currently pursuing my PhD at the University of Limerick within the I-Form Research Centre for Advanced Manufacturing, I investigate Low-Code/No-Code (LC/NC) architectures and Digital Threads to drive digitalization across manufacturing lifecycles.

My background spans lecturing, curriculum leadership, and hands-on software development. Prior to Ireland, I served as a Lecturer and Program Coordinator at Jimma University, Ethiopia, where I advised students, led accreditation efforts, and built systems ranging from mobile health apps to AI-based agricultural diagnostics.
