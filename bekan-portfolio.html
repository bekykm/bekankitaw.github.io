About

I am a Computer Scientist and academic researcher currently pursuing a PhD at the University of Limerick. My work focuses on bridging Artificial Intelligence with industrial systems through Digital Threads and Low-Code/No-Code architectures.


Prior to my PhD, I worked as a Lecturer and Program Coordinator at Jimma University, where I taught software engineering, AI, databases, and networks, and supervised multiple student research projects.
